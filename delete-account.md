# Account Deletion Process

If you wish to delete your account, please follow these instructions:

1. **Request Account Deletion**: Send an email to support@partyspace.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". Please include your username and any additional identifier that may be useful in processing your request.
2. **Data Involved**: The following data will be deleted upon account deletion:
   - User profile information
   - Transaction history
   - Any uploaded content
3. **Timeline for Deletion**: Upon receiving your deletion request, we aim to complete the process within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been successfully deleted.

For more information, you can visit our [Privacy Policy](https://www.partyspace.com/privacy).

Thank you for choosing PartySpace! We're sad to see you go!
