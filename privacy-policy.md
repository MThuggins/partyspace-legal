# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: March 11, 2026_

**PartySpace** ("we", "us", "our") provides a platform for party service providers and venues to connect with individuals seeking to rent spaces and related services. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

## Information We Collect

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, password.
- **Profile Details**: Venue/service details, images, location.
- **Usage Data**: App interactions, preferences, device information.
- **Booking Information**: Dates, services requested, communication details.

## How We Use Your Information

- To create and manage your account.
- To facilitate bookings between service providers, venues, and users.
- To communicate important updates and respond to inquiries.
- To improve PartySpace's features, safety, and user experience.

## Sharing Your Information

We only share necessary information between venues/providers and clients to facilitate bookings. We do not sell user data to third parties.

## Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect your information.

## Your Choices

You may update or delete your account information at any time. Contact us at support@partyspace.com for additional privacy questions.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via email or app notifications.
